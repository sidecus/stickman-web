Privacy Policy

Jackie's Stickman War · Effective June 22, 2026

Jackie's Stickman War does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information. The game runs locally on your device and does not require an account or an internet connection.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information, gameplay data, device identifiers, location, contacts, photos, or any other user data.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The app does not include analytics services, advertising networks, tracking technology, or third-party software development kits that collect data. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Local Operation and Data Retention

All game logic, graphics, and sound run locally within the app. Because we do not receive or store user data, there is no personal data for us to retain, sell, disclose, or delete. iOS and WebKit may manage temporary technical files on the device as part of normal system operation; the developer does not access those files.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any other users.

Apple Services

Apple may process App Store, download, purchase, or diagnostic information independently under Apple's own privacy policies. That processing is controlled by Apple, not by Jackie's Stickman War.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change, this policy will be updated before those changes take effect. The effective date above identifies the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Jackie's Stickman War support page. Please do not include personal or sensitive information in a public issue.
